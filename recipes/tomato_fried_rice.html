Tomato Fried Rice

Description

A simple stir-fry that anyone can cook up using common asian household ingredients

Ingredients

  • cooked white rice (preferably cooked the night before)
  • sliced chicken, bacon can be used for better flavour
  • 2 whole tomatoes, diced
  • 1 garlic clove, chopped
  • spring onion, sliced thinly -optional-
  • cooking oil
  • ketchup, 2 tablespoons
  • soy sauce
  • salt

Steps

  1. Heat up oil in your wok or pan
  2. Throw in the chopped garlic, followed by sliced meat
  3. Add ketchup and diced tomatoes to the pan, and cook on low heat, until soft and juices are released
  4. Throw in the white rice and stir-fry vigorously
  5. Season the rice with a teaspoon of salt, and a generous dash of soy sauce, until a light golden colour is seen
  6. Serve with sliced spring onion garnished on top
