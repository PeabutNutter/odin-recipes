Garlic Fried Rice

Description

A simple stir fry that anyone can cook up using common asian household ingredients

Ingredients

  • cooked white rice (preferably cooked the night before)
  • sliced chicken, bacon can be used for better flavour
  • 1 garlic clove, chopped
  • spring onion, sliced thinly -optional-
  • cooking oil
  • soy sauce
  • salt

Steps

  1. Heat up oil in your wok or pan
  2. Throw in the chopped garlic, followed by sliced meat
  3. Throw in the white rice and stir-fry vigorously
  4. Season the rice with a teaspoon of salt, and a generous dash of soy sauce, until a light golden colour is seen
  5. Serve with sliced spring onion garnished on top
